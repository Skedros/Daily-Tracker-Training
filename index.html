Section 07
Call Outcome Definitions
Always select one. Never leave it blank.
Booked Follow-Up
You agreed on a specific next step — a scheduled call, promised email, or date to reconnect.
"He asked me to call back Tuesday." / "Sent CC email."
Left Voicemail
You reached voicemail and left a message. No live conversation happened.
"Called, got voicemail, left a message."
Not Interested
They said no or are unqualified. Move to marketing only in Salesforce.
"NGO, moved to marketing."
Moved to Active
A real deal is in progress. Financials exchanged or term sheet being prepared.
"Financials received, sending to credit."
Proposal Sent
You sent a formal proposal or term sheet today as a direct result of this call.
"Emailed full proposal package after this call."
Other
Always fill in the description. Never leave blank.
"Referred to colleague." / "Transaction discussion."
